Why Inflation Feels Higher Than the Headline Number

Inflation is often summarized as one annual percentage. But households do not spend evenly across all goods and services. When essential categories rise faster than the overall basket, inflation can feel harsher than the official number suggests.

Canada 2011–2024 Year-over-Year Inflation

How to Read This Visualization

  • The chart shows annual inflation rates, not total price growth since a base year.
  • A value of 5% in 2022 means that category was about 5% more expensive than in 2021.
  • Official inflation is the all-items CPI rate. Your inflation is a weighted estimate based on your spending mix.
